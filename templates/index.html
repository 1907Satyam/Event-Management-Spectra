About Spectra

Spectra is the annual cultural event proudly organized by the 3rd-year students of our institution. The student organizing committee has designed this two-day college fest to provide a vibrant platform for everyone to showcase their talents.

Our Core Objectives:

  • To encourage student creativity and talent.
  • To promote cultural and recreational activities on campus.
  • To develop teamwork, leadership, and management skills among students.
  • To create a lively and engaging campus environment.

Theme: Mystic India

This theme represents the rich cultural heritage, vibrant colours, traditions, and diversity of India. Mystic India highlights the beauty of Indian art, music, dance, fashion, and festivals. The theme celebrates unity in diversity and brings together various cultural expressions into one spectacular, vibrant event.
